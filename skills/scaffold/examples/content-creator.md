# Example: Content Creator

## Profile
A solo content creator producing blog posts, newsletters, social media, and video scripts. Monetizes through courses, sponsorships, and affiliate marketing.

## Employees (Agents)
1. **Writer** - Drafts blog posts, newsletters, scripts from outlines
2. **Editor** - Reviews drafts for clarity, grammar, tone, and engagement
3. **SEO Specialist** - Keyword research, meta tags, internal linking, content optimization
4. **Social Media Manager** - Repurposes content into platform-specific posts, schedules

## SOPs (Skills)
1. `/content:brainstorm` - Generate content ideas for a topic or niche
2. `/content:outline` - Create a structured outline for a piece of content
3. `/content:write` - Draft content from an outline
4. `/content:edit` - Review and improve a draft
5. `/content:seo` - Optimize content for search engines
6. `/content:repurpose` - Turn a blog post into tweets, LinkedIn posts, newsletter
7. `/content:schedule` - Plan a content calendar for the week/month

## Team Meetings (Agent Teams)
1. **Content Sprint**: Writer + SEO Specialist + Editor (produce a full post)
2. **Distribution Team**: Social Media Manager + Writer (repurpose + schedule)

## Tools (MCP Servers)
- Notion (content calendar, drafts)
- WordPress or Ghost (publishing)
- Google Search Console (SEO data)
- Buffer or Hootsuite (social scheduling)

## Lifecycle
`/brainstorm` → `/outline` → `/write` → `/edit` → `/seo` → `/repurpose` → `/schedule`
